Welcome

     I am Berta, a qualified psychotherapist and counsellor, offering in-person sessions in Poulton-le-Fylde, as well as online and telephone counselling services across the UK. I provide a compassionate and confidential environment, carefully tailored to meet your individual needs.
     I offer support for a wide range of challenges, including anxiety, depression, trauma, self-esteem and confidence issues, bereavement, divorce, body image concerns, domestic abuse, sexuality, loneliness, and identity issues. Additionally, I specialize in helping individuals navigate difficulties within relationships, whether dealing with betrayal, marital challenges, family dynamics, dating, or other interpersonal conflicts.
