     I am Berta, a qualified psychotherapist and counsellor offering in-person sessions in Poulton-le-Fylde, as well as online video and telephone counselling across the UK. I specialise in working with men who feel stuck, overwhelmed, or disconnected, men who struggle to open up, process emotions, or find balance in their lives. I offer a safe, grounded, confidential, and judgement-free space where you can explore what is really going on beneath the surface, without being told how to feel or pressured to change immediately.

     Many men are taught to push feelings down, stay strong, and deal with challenges alone. Over time, this can show up as stress, anxiety, low mood, anger, emotional numbness, or difficulties in relationships. In the UK, men are three times more likely to die by suicide than women, and suicide remains the leading cause of death for men under 50 (ONS, 2023). Yet men are significantly less likely to seek psychological support or talk about how they feel.

     I support men with challenges such as anxiety, depression, anger, stress, confidence issues, identity questions, relationship difficulties, trauma, relational trauma, loneliness, separation and divorce, sexuality, body image, loss and grief, and difficulties processing emotions. If you have ever felt like you cannot talk about your feelings, therapy can help you develop emotional awareness, build stronger relationships, and reconnect with yourself in a practical and grounded way. I also have extensive experience supporting men affected by substance use and navigating major life transitions, stress, burnout, or loss of direction.

     You do not need to have the right words or a clear explanation for how you feel. If you know something is not working and you are tired of carrying it alone, therapy can help you make sense of what is going on and move towards meaningful change.
